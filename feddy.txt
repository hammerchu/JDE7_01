Ingredients:
Momofuku Steamed Tofu - 1 box
Lee Kum Kee Mapo Tofu Sauce - 1 pack
Minced pork - 1 box
Chopped green onion - 1 tsp

Steps:
1. Fry minced pork with 1-2 tbsp oil.

2. Add silken tofu and Lee Kum Kee Mapo Tofu Sauce, mix well with hands.

3. Cook over low heat until the sauce thickens, then serve on a plate and sprinkle with chopped green onion.
